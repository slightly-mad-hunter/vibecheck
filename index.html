📖 How to Export from Each App
📱 MyFitnessPal ›
Go to Settings → Export Data. Choose a date range and tap Export to CSV. Email it to yourself, then open here. MFP exports nutrition, exercise, and weight data.
⌚ Samsung Health ›
Open Samsung Health → tap your profile icon → Export Personal Data. It exports as a zip of CSV files. Unzip on your PC, then import each CSV file here (steps, sleep, heart rate, etc.).
🏥 Practice Better ›
Ask your practitioner to export your food/symptom logs, or go to your client portal → Food Journal → Export. Import the CSV here under "Generic CSV".
🌸 Clue / Flo (Cycle) ›
Clue: Settings → Export Data → CSV. Flo: Profile → Settings → Export Data. Both export cycle, symptoms, and mood data that maps into the Cycle tracker here.
🤕 Migraine / Headache Apps ›
Most migraine apps (Migraine Buddy, Headache Diary) have a Reports → Export CSV option. Export your log and select "Migraine Tracker" as the source when importing here.
